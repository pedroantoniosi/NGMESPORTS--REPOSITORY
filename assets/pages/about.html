Origins

NGM Virtual Racing Team was founded in 2010 by New Generation Mobile, with a vision to create a competitive virtual racing team that would dominate the fast-growing world of eSports. Combining cutting-edge technology and a passion for motorsport, NGM eSports quickly made its mark in the virtual racing scene. The team’s creation was driven by the belief that the future of racing was not only on the track but also in the digital world.

History

Over the years, NGM eSports has competed in some of the most prestigious virtual racing leagues across the globe, earning a reputation as one of the leading teams in the industry. From high-speed circuits to grueling endurance events, NGM eSports raced in nearly every major online competition. Notably, the team achieved remarkable success in the IndyCar e-Championship, winning the title twice and cementing their place as one of the top virtual racing teams.

Their rise to prominence was fueled by a talented roster of drivers, cutting-edge racing simulators, and a strategic approach that balanced speed with precision. As the team gained more recognition, it became a symbol of excellence in virtual motorsport.

Departure from virtual racing

Despite their dominance, NGM eSports made the difficult decision to step away from virtual racing for a period of time. In 2019, after a decade of intense competition, the team chose to take a break from the virtual racing scene to refocus and explore new opportunities. The departure marked the end of an era, but it also set the stage for a new chapter in the team’s history.

Return

In 2024, after years of absence, NGM eSports made a triumphant return to virtual racing. Reinvigorated and with a renewed sense of purpose, the team is back on the track, ready to reclaim its spot at the top of the eSports racing world. With new talent, state-of-the-art technology, and the same competitive drive that first defined them, NGM eSports is poised to continue its legacy of excellence in the world of virtual motorsport.
